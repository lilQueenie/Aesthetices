Being Aesthetic

Color Palette: Choose a cohesive color scheme for your outfits, social media posts, or living space. Stick to a few key colors that complement each other.

Consistent Theme: Maintain a consistent theme or mood in your content or surroundings. This could be minimalist, vintage, modern, or any other style that resonates with you.

Composition and Symmetry: Pay attention to how elements are arranged. Clean lines and balanced compositions contribute to a more aesthetically pleasing look.

Quality Photography: Whether it's for your Instagram feed or personal collections, use high-quality and well-composed photographs. Good lighting and framing make a significant difference.

What Style!

Mindful Fashion Choices: Build a wardrobe with pieces that match your aesthetic. Pay attention to the details, such as fabric, patterns, and silhouettes.

Typography and Design: If you're creating digital content, pay attention to typography and design. Consistent fonts and well-designed layouts enhance the overall aesthetic.

Nature and Natural Elements: Incorporate natural elements, plants, or outdoor scenes into your aesthetic. Nature-inspired aesthetics often evoke a sense of tranquility and beauty.

Personal Expression: Ultimately, your aesthetic is a form of self-expression. Choose elements that resonate with you personally, and don't be afraid to experiment and evolve over time.
